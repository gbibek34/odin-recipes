Pancakes

Description

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter, and then cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. It is a type of batter bread.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Start by preparing the batter for pancake. Take a cup of flour, add sugar and salt to your taste, add a teaspoon of baking powder and mix
  2. Further, add a cup of milk and start whisking the mixture leaving no air bubbles. Add eggs and vanilla extract and start whisking again until you have a smooth batter ready to go.
  3. Take a pan and heat it, add some butter to it (enough for a pancake to cook).
  4. Pour in a small amount of batter (According to the size of pancake you want) and let it cook until it bubbles upwards and the runny batter start to solidify.
  5. Flip the pancake once it has no runny batter on top and cook it until both the sides start looking light brown
  6. Finally stack the pancakes and add in some honey or butter on top of them. You can enjoy them with some dry fruits or berries on the side.
