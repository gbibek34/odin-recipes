Carrot Pudding

Description

The carrot pudding is a wonderful dessert enjoyed and found mostly in nepal and india. Using the flavors from different aromatic spices and the volume from milk and carrot the carrot-pudding is a really simple and quick dessert to make when you want to enjoy something sweet that melts inside your mouth.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Green Cardamom
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare you carrots in the beginning by washing, peeling and chopping them into decent sized pieces.
  2. Start by heating the cookware you want to use that will fit the amount of carrots and milk you want to use.
  3. Put in the ghee and let it heat and melt until a faint smell of ghee starts to spread.
  4. Break the cinnamon stick into small pieces and fry them in the ghee.
  5. Add the other spices we took out -> Cloves, Green Cardamom and Black Pepper(Ground them properly.)
  6. After the cloves start to spurt out the ghee add in the chopped carrots.
  7. Let the carrots fry in the ghee for a while and then add sugar according to your liking.
  8. When the sugar starts to caramelize add in the milk
  9. Cover the cookware and let it cook until the carrots are boiled enough to smash and the milk is reduced as well
  10. Finally, smash the carrots and add in some dry fruits like cashews and almonds
  11. Store the hot carrot pudding in a container and keep it in the fridge until its cold
