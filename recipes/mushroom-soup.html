Mushroom Soup

Description

The mushroom soup is a delicious meal that will fit in your diet if you want something healthy without saddening your taste buds. It can be used as a side curry with some rice or chapatis.

Ingredients

  • Mushroom
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Green Chillies
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Coriander and Cumin Powder
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Start by preparing your ingredients, chop the potatoes, mushrooms, onions, chillies and tomatoes and get the ready to jump in some oil.
  2. Heat up a cookware of your choice (Make sure it has a lid or you have something to cover it for cooking) and pour oil according to the quantity of ingredients you have chopped.
  3. Begin by throwing in the onions and green chillies and let them caramelize for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add in the potatoes and let them fry until the potatoes turn somewhat golden in color.
  5. Add in the ginger garlic paste, a pinch or two of turmeric powder and coriander and cumin powder according to your taste.
  6. Add the mushrooms, stir them a little, add salt according to your taste and let everything cook for a while by covering the cookware with the heat at low-medium.
  7. Then when the mushrooms are somewhat cooked add in the tomatoes and let it cook for a while by covering it.
  8. After the tomatoes seem to be half cooked add in some water (You can decide the quantity according to your ingredients) and let it cook for 3-5 minutes more.
  9. Finally, you have your delicious mushroom potatoes soup that you can enjoy on a rainy cold day while resting beside your window.
